ParallelCook iOS App Released

hovvy is pleased to announce the release of "ParallelCook".

"ParallelCook" is a cooking support tool that manages your personal recipes and helps you prepare multiple dishes at once. AI analyzes recipes to optimize cooking steps so even those not confident in the kitchen won't get lost. It navigates "the most efficient procedure to prepare 3 dishes at once", dramatically streamlining your time in the kitchen.

Key Features:

  • Recipe Management - Register and manage your own recipes
  • AI Cooking Optimization - Analyzes recipes to suggest optimal cooking procedures
  • Parallel Cooking Navigation - Guides you through preparing multiple dishes simultaneously
  • Time Efficiency - Maximizes the effectiveness of your time in the kitchen
