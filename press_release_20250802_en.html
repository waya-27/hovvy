Shopping Management App "Areatta?" Released

hovvy is pleased to announce the release of "Areatta?", a new shopping management app.

"Areatta?" is a shopping partner app that helps you manage what you have at home and what you need at a glance, eliminating forgotten purchases and duplicate buying. It smartly manages your condiment and ingredient inventory, and by checking "items you're running low on" and switching to shopping mode, it makes your supermarket shopping more efficient.

Key Features:

  • Inventory Management - Simply manage the "have/don't have" status of condiments and ingredients at home
  • Shopping Mode - Check items you're low on and easily switch to shopping list screen
  • One-Tap Update - Simply tap purchased items to automatically update your inventory list
  • Intuitive Operation - Simple UI design that anyone can use easily

"Areatta?" is a tool designed to make your daily shopping smarter and more comfortable. It prevents forgotten purchases and unnecessary duplicate buying, supporting efficient household budget management.
