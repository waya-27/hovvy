Golf Practice Tracking App "NiceShot Log" Released

hovvy is pleased to announce the release of "NiceShot Log", a new golf practice tracking app.

"NiceShot Log" is an app that digitizes your driving range practice and visualizes club-by-club improvement. It transforms monotonous practice into a strategic game, accurately recording nice shot rates and average distances for each club to support efficient skill development.

Key Features:

  • Club-Specific Data Recording - Accurately track nice shot rates and average distances for each club
  • SIM ROUND Mode - Practice switching clubs as you would in actual rounds to efficiently overcome weaknesses
  • Data Visualization - Display accumulated data in easy-to-understand graphs to track your progress
  • Strategic Club Selection - Practice data powerfully supports logical club choices on actual courses

"NiceShot Log" transforms monotonous driving range practice into a strategic and enjoyable game, enabling efficient improvement based on data. It's the perfect app for anyone who wants to improve their golf skills via the fastest route.
