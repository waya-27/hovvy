Launched Lifelog App "Shumi no Shirushi"

hovvy has released a new lifelog app called "Shumi no Shirushi" (Signs of Hobbies).

"Shumi no Shirushi" is an app that supports loose connections and motivation to help you continue enjoying your hobbies and habits. It allows you to know the number of hobby companions in a completely private manner, providing a sense of security that you're not alone while protecting your privacy.

Key Features:

  • Completely Private Records - Keep your personal lifelog without anyone seeing it
  • Loose Connections - Know only the "number" of people who share the same hobbies
  • Motivation Maintenance - Stay motivated through continuous recording
  • Privacy Focused - No personal information is ever made public
