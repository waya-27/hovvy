Area Tracking Running App "Arealist" Released

hovvy is pleased to announce the release of "Arealist", a new area tracking running app.

"Arealist" is a revolutionary app that tracks the "area" you've covered while running. It offers a new adventure experience of finding roads you've never traveled and painting the world. It transforms exercise "monotony" into "exploration", and the sense of achievement from painting unexplored paths adds excitement to your daily runs and travels.

Key Features:

  • Area-Based Recording - A new perspective that tracks "area" covered instead of just distance
  • Paint the Earth - Visualize paths you've traveled by painting them on the map, creating a sense of achievement
  • Exploration Motivation - The joy of discovering unexplored roads turns daily exercise into an adventure
  • Global Data Integration - Connect with anonymous data from runners worldwide to collectively hack the Earth

"Arealist" is not just a running tracking app. It transforms exercise into an exploration game, bringing new discoveries and a sense of achievement to monotonous daily routines. Join the adventure of painting the world.
