Official YouTube Channel Launched

hovvy is pleased to announce the launch of our official YouTube channel.

On this channel, we will be publishing videos that explain in an easy-to-understand way the mechanics and applications of AI orchestration — one of hovvy's core areas of expertise. Our content will cover how to build workflows that connect multiple AI models to automate business operations, real-world implementation examples, and technical deep dives.

If you are interested in improving operations with AI or want to stay up to date with the latest AI technology, please subscribe to our channel so you never miss a new video.

Planned Content:

  • AI Orchestration Explained - How to build workflows that connect multiple AI models
  • Autonomous Task Automation Examples - Real cases of using AI to automate judgment-based routine work
  • Behind the Scenes of AI App Development - Insights into hovvy's development process and design philosophy
