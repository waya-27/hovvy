ParallelCook

Manage your own recipes and support tools for parallel cooking. AI analyzes recipes to optimize cooking steps so even those not confident in the kitchen won't get lost. It navigates 'the most efficient procedure to prepare 3 dishes at once', dramatically streamlining your time in the kitchen.

NiceShot Log

Track your nice shot rate and average distance for each club with precision. Use 'SUN ROUND mode', which simulates actual rounds by switching clubs, to efficiently overcome your weaknesses. Accumulated data strongly supports logical club selection on the real course.

Arealist

A new sensation app to record 'areas'. The satisfaction of filling in unexplored paths adds spice to everyday running and travel. Connect with anonymous data from runners worldwide and hack the planet together with all users.

hobby ripple

Connect loosely and turn daily life into 'habits'. A life-log app where you privately see how many hobby companions you have to boost motivation. Record not just sports or outdoor activities, but every casual moment—sunbathing, napping, coffee time—and make them all count.
